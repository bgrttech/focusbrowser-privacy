🛡️ Privacy Policy

Focus Browser  ·  Effective Date: January 1, 2025

✅ Our Privacy Promise

  • We do not collect any personal data
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not share any information with third parties
  • Everything stays on your device — always
  • No accounts, no sign-ups, no cloud sync
  • No analytics or advertising SDKs included

1. Introduction

Focus Browser ("we", "our", or "the app") is a privacy-first Android browser developed for personal productivity. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information. By using Focus Browser, you agree to the practices described here.

The short version: we collect nothing. Every piece of data the app generates — your history, bookmarks, downloads, and settings — is stored exclusively on your local device and never leaves it.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

Focus Browser does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Browsing history or visited URLs
  • Search queries
  • Bookmarks or saved pages
  • Downloaded file names or contents
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Android ID, etc.)
  • IP address or location data
  • Cookies or session data
  • Keystrokes or form inputs
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

3. Local Data Storage On-Device Only

Some data is stored locally on your device solely to provide the app's functionality. This includes:

  • Browsing history — stored locally, viewable and deletable by you at any time
  • Bookmarks — saved on-device, never synced externally
  • App settings and preferences — stored in local SharedPreferences
  • Downloaded files — saved to your device's local storage
  • Tab session — temporarily stored locally to restore open tabs

All of this data remains exclusively on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

4. Internet Access

Focus Browser requires internet access to function as a web browser — to load the websites you choose to visit. This is entirely under your control.

The app itself does not make any background network requests, phone home to any server, or send any data over the internet on your behalf.

5. Third-Party Websites

When you browse the web, you may visit third-party websites that have their own privacy policies. Focus Browser is not responsible for the privacy practices of any website you visit.

Focus Browser's built-in Shield feature actively blocks ads, trackers, and cookie banners on websites to help protect your privacy while browsing.

6. App Permissions

Focus Browser requests only the permissions necessary for core functionality:

  • Internet — to load web pages you navigate to
  • Storage / Media — to save files you choose to download
  • Camera / Microphone — only if a website you visit requests it; you are always prompted before access is granted

No permission is used to collect or transmit data to us or any third party.

7. Children's Privacy

Focus Browser does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data from anyone, no special data handling for children is required. The app is safe to use for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
